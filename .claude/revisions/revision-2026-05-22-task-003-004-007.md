## TASK-004 — Wake lock

### Changes
- Updated: `data/FarmForegroundService.kt`
  - Added `PowerManager.PARTIAL_WAKE_LOCK` with 4h timeout
  - `acquireWakeLock()` / `releaseWakeLock()` — idempotent, try-catch, logged
  - Acquire: `onStartCommand()` after `startForeground()`. Release: `onDestroy()` + `ACTION_STOP`
